Biography

I would like to share one of my Favorite food recipe: Creamy chocolate Ingredients: 2 cups of milk (tea) of milk 1 tablespoon cornstarch of chocolate powder 3 tablespoons cocoa powder 4 tablespoons of sugar 1 cinnamon stick 1 box of milk cream Prepare: In a blender, beat the milk, cornstarch, chocolate powder and sugar. Pour the mixture into a pan with the cinnamon and lead to low fire, stirring constantly until it boils. Turn off, add the cream of milk and stir well until you get a homogeneous mix. Remove the cinnamon and serve hot. P.S.: Creamy chocolate is excellent, specially in cold days. The cinnamon is optional, so you decide if you want to put it or not in the chocolate.
